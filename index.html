🌟 The Founder's Story

A Journey from Zero to the Zenith

I was born on the sacred soil where the legendary Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj took birth — Junnar (Shivneri), Pune. Growing up in the shadow of Shivneri Fort, I inherited the spirit of 'Swarajya' — the will to build something of my own and never back down from a challenge. Carrying those values, I moved to the city and completed my graduation at the prestigious Ness Wadia College, Pune.

But my destiny wasn't in a 9-to-5 desk job. My eyes were always fixed on the fluctuating numbers and the rhythm of the candlestick charts.

"The Stock Market is not just a place to make money; it is a test of your character and patience. My mission is not just to make you a 'Trader,' but to lead you toward 'Financial Sovereignty'."

— Vilas Ware

The Turning Point: My trading journey began in 2015. It wasn't an overnight success story. It was a path paved with sleepless nights, market crashes, and the harsh lessons of loss. To master the "language of money," I spent two crucial years (2017 to 2019) working with India's leading brokerage house, Motilal Oswal. That experience was my real-world university. It taught me the secrets of the 'Big Bulls' — how the institutional players move and why retail traders often lose their hard-earned money.

The Mission: The Ware Group
After seeing thousands of people lose their savings due to a lack of guidance and high-risk gambling in the markets, a thought struck me: "If I have mastered the art of survival and profit, why not empower others?"

With this vision, I began teaching the art of trading in 2021. Today, I am incredibly proud that The Ware Group has successfully built an army of over 500+ profitable students who are now trading with discipline and confidence.

Join me at The Ware Group. Let's decode the market together and turn your financial dreams into a living reality. Because in this market, you only earn when you learn.

Vilas Ware
Founder, The Ware Group
